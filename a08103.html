Detailed Description

A range of dates.

The elements in this range are Date objects. Consequently the precision of

DateRange

objects is milliseconds.

Author
Martin Desruisseaux (Geomatys)
Version
3.20
See also
org.geotoolkit.measure.RangeFormat
