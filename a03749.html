Detailed Description

Extensions to standard Java java.io.Reader and java.io.Writer for I/O operations.

Contains also a few java.text.Format implementations that are expected to be used in close relationship with readers or writers.

Many

Writers

defined in this package are actually java.io.FilterWriter used for applying on-the-fly formatting while writing text to the output device. For example org.geotoolkit.io.IndentedLineWriter adds indentation at the beginning of every new line, and org.geotoolkit.io.TableWriter replaces all occurrence of

'\t'

by the amount of spaces needed for producing a tabular output.
